The Double-Edged Screen

A digital research publication analyzing how social media is transforming the lives of youth in rural India.

Role: Lead Researcher & Data Visualizer
Date: 2025
Tech Stack: Data Analysis, D3.js, Chart.js
Status: Ongoing

Key Findings

The core challenge has shifted from providing internet access to building digital resilienceâ€”teaching youth how to navigate the online world safely and effectively.

The Promise: Opportunities

  • Borderless Classroom: YouTube and other platforms act as vital educational tools.
  • Skill Building: Rise of "nano-entrepreneurs" using platforms like Instagram for business.
  • Social Connection: A lifeline for those in isolated areas to find support and reduce loneliness.

The Perils: Risks

  • Mental Health Crisis: Strong links to increased anxiety, depression, and poor self-esteem (FOMO).
  • Algorithmic Addiction: Platforms designed for addiction lead to poor focus and academic decline.
  • A Hostile Web: Youth are often unprepared for cyberbullying and other online dangers.

The Path Forward

Policymakers

Integrate digital resilience (online safety, misinformation, mental health) into the national school curriculum.

Tech Companies

Redesign platforms to prioritize user well-being over engagement and invest in moderation for regional languages.

Educators & Parents

Foster open communication and work together to establish healthy boundaries for device usage.
