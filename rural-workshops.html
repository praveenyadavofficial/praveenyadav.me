Rural Outreach Workshops

A social initiative to conduct tech-literacy and awareness workshops in underserved rural areas.

Role: Program Coordinator & Facilitator
Date: 2025
Focus: Youth Empowerment, Community Impact
Status: Ongoing
Download Report

Project Overview

This ongoing outreach program is dedicated to bridging the digital divide by bringing hands-on technology education to students in rural villages. The workshops are designed to be interactive, engaging, and relevant to the local context, moving beyond basic computer skills to inspire curiosity and confidence in using technology for learning, creativity, and future career opportunities.

Workshop Highlights

  • Wide Reach: Successfully conducted workshops in over 10 rural villages, reaching hundreds of students.
  • Diverse Curriculum: Topics range from internet safety and identifying misinformation to introductions to coding concepts and digital art.
  • Community-Led: The program is run in collaboration with local educators and student volunteers, ensuring the content is culturally relevant and sustainable.
  • Hands-On Learning: We prioritize interactive sessions, group activities, and feedback loops over traditional lectures to maximize engagement and knowledge retention.
