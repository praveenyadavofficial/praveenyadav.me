People of India Magazine

A 15-page digital magazine series giving a voice to the everyday heroes who form the backbone of India's growth.

Role: Editorial Designer & Storyteller
Date: 2025
Tools: Adobe Creative Suite, Photography
Status: Publishing Soon
Read The Magazine

Project Overview

The "People of India" magazine is a personal passion project aimed at celebrating the dignity of labor and the human stories that often go unheard. The series features in-depth interviews with auto drivers, street vendors, artisans, and other unsung professionals. The goal was to move beyond statistics and headlines to showcase their vital role in society through compelling, first-person storytelling and professional portrait photography.

Highlights of the Series

  • In-depth Interviews: Each feature is built around a detailed interview, allowing subjects to share their experiences, challenges, and aspirations in their own words.
  • Original Photography: Every story is accompanied by a series of high-quality portraits and environmental shots to capture the personality and context of the individual.
  • Interactive Digital Format: Designed as a digital-first publication, the magazine features a clean, readable layout optimized for screens, with potential for future interactive elements.
  • Multi-language Accessibility: The content is planned to be translated into multiple regional languages to ensure it reaches the communities it represents.
