About

I'm a developer passionate about crafting accessible, pixel-perfect user interfaces that blend thoughtful design with robust engineering. My favorite work lies at the intersection of design and development, creating experiences that not only look great but are meticulously built for performance and usability.

Currently, I'm a freelance frontend developer and UI/UX designer, specializing in creating meaningful digital experiences. I work with startups, NGOs, and creative agencies to build platforms that make a real difference in people's lives.

In the past, I've had the opportunity to develop digital solutions across various domains — from civic-tech platforms and research publications to creative agencies and video content creation. Additionally, I've contributed to community initiatives and educational projects focused on digital literacy and social impact.

When I'm not at the computer, I'm usually exploring new design trends, learning about emerging technologies, or working on community projects that bridge the digital divide.
